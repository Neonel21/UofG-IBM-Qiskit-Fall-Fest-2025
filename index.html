University of Glasgow IBM Qiskit Fall Fest

Welcome to the Qiskit Fall Fest at the UofG! We're so excited to have you all here. Qiskit Fall Fest is a collection of quantum computing events on college campuses and institutions around the world, supported and endorsed by IBM Quantum.

Register

Immerse Yourself in the World of Quantum Computing

Join us for a unique workshop co-hosted by IBM Quantum, where early and learning are our top priorities. Get ready to dive into the fascinating world of quantum computing with hands-on sessions, expert talks, and networking opportunities! This event is designed to provide an interactive and engaging experience for all participants.
